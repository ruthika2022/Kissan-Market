Namaste! My name is Samir Dombe, and I am the proud owner of Pavitarak Farmer Producer Company and Pavitarak Farm. I am a 32-year-old second-generation farmer from Daund, Pune, and I hold a B.E. degree in Medical. Farming has been a part of my family tradition, and I am proud to continue it with dedication and innovation. When I started farming, I focused on cultivating anjer (figs), which is my main crop even today. Farming has been a continuous learning journey for me, and I still learn new skills and techniques every day. Kisan Market supports progressive farmers like me by providing a digital platform that connects farmers directly with buyers, helping us reach wider markets and gain better value for our produce.

Farming comes with many challenges, such as ensuring proper crop growth and handling fluctuating market prices. To overcome these challenges, I adopted modern farming technologies and improved farm management practices. On my farm, I use tractors, HTP pumps, brush cutters, and fine irrigation methods to increase efficiency and productivity. I prioritize the use of organic seeds and follow sustainable farming practices to protect the environment. Kisan Market encourages such eco-friendly and modern farming approaches by helping farmers understand market demand, set fair prices, and reduce dependency on middlemen. Farming in my family is a joint effort—my two brothers, my wife, and all family members actively contribute to the farm’s operations, working together with unity and commitment.

My main crop, anjer (figs), is not only highly nutritious but also profitable and widely used in dishes such as anjer milkshake and anjer halwa. My daily routine starts at 6 a.m. and continues until 10 p.m., reflecting the dedication required in farming. The most satisfying part of farming for me is watching the crops grow well and knowing that my efforts are yielding results. My farm operates with a team of 27 dedicated workers, and I earn an annual income of around 35 lakhs. Daund, located on the banks of the Bhima River, is rich in history and surrounded by famous attractions like Bhigwan Bird Sanctuary and Mayureshwar Wildlife Sanctuary. During the off-season, I focus on learning new farming methods, visiting friends, and seeking guidance from agricultural advisors. In conclusion, Kisan Market acts as a strong support system for modern farmers, helping us grow sustainably, earn better income, and deliver high-quality produce directly to consumers while preserving the pride of Indian agriculture.
